WP3: Empathic Listening Training

Training for Youth Workers & Operators

Strengthening professional capacities to foster inclusion and prevent radicalization

Type

International intensive training workshop with scenario-based learning and interactive methodologies

Coordination

Marta - Parsec Cooperativa Sociale (Italy)

Duration

5 days (4 training days + 1 travel day)

Participants

4 per country: 3 youth workers + 1 coordinator/trainer

Venue

Netherlands (hosted by Changes&Chances)

Timeline

June - July 2026

Training Topics & Content

1 Empathetic Listening & Trauma-Aware Communication

Building trust with young migrants through empathetic listening techniques, recognizing early signs of marginalization and disengagement, trauma-informed approaches to support vulnerable youth

2 Peer Education & Youth Participation

Facilitating peer-to-peer learning processes, empowering young migrants as active participants and co-creators, developing peer educator teams for sustainable inclusion strategies

3 Intercultural Mediation & Conflict Navigation

Enhancing intercultural communication and dialogue facilitation, conflict resolution and mediation techniques, bridging cultural differences in reception services

4 Safeguarding, Case Management & Referral (incl. UAMs)

Understanding radicalization dynamics and risk factors, implementing safeguarding protocols for vulnerable youth, effective case management and referral pathways for Unaccompanied Minors
